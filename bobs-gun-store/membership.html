Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?

Visit our store or call us at 519-999-9191. We'll get you set up with a membership agreement and payment information. Memberships are month-to-month with no long-term contracts.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes! You can change your membership tier at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What documents do I need?

You'll need a valid ID and a PAL (Possession and Acquisition License) to join. Beginners without a PAL can book supervised range sessions.

Are guest passes transferable?

Yes! Guest passes can be used by any guest you bring to the range. They're included with Silver and Gold memberships.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There's no long-term commitment. Simply notify us 7 days before the end of your billing cycle to cancel.

Ready to join?

Visit us in person or call to start your membership today.
