About Bob's Gun Store

Serving Waterloo with integrity and expertise since 2008.

Our Story

Bob's Gun Store was founded with a simple mission: to provide the Waterloo community with premium firearms, knowledgeable staff, and a safe, professional shooting range. What started as a small retail operation has grown into the region's most trusted firearms destination.

Our commitment to safety, education, and customer service has made us the go-to choice for both seasoned shooters and first-time buyers. We believe in responsible firearm ownership and provide comprehensive training for all skill levels.
